How to Connect
Step 1 — Instagram: Your account must be a Business or Creator account linked to a Facebook Page. Personal accounts are not supported by the Instagram Graph API.

Step 2 — YouTube: Sign in with the Google account that owns the channel. The connection uses read-only YouTube Analytics scopes — we never post on your behalf.

Step 3 — Meta Ads: You need Meta Business Manager access with at least Analyst-level permissions on the ad accounts you want to monitor.

All connections are read-only. KAMOD AI never posts, schedules, or modifies anything on your accounts.
OAuth integration coming soon — API keys will be configured in Settings once ready.
